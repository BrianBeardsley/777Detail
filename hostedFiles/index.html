Detailing Services

Automotive detailing is about more than just washing your car, at 777 we offer a wide range of products and services to give your vehicle the attention and care it deserves. From detailing and restoration packages to protective and customization options 777 is your local detailing expert. Contact us today and speak with a team member to book your next appointment (Contact).

  • The 777 Package

    • A full detail and deep cleaning package using top of the line automotive detailing products.

    • Interior Care

      • Upholstery and carpet cleaned and shampooed including a stain removal process
      • All surfaces are cleaned and detailed using industry leading automotive detailing products
      • Doorjambs (Including trunk and rear-hatches) are deep cleaned and restored
      • Fragrance and odor treatment with a variety of choices
      • Optional: Protection treatments for carpet, upholstery, vinyl, plastic and leather surfaces

    • Exterior care

      • Comprehensive exterior cleaning and detail using high quality products and a gentle touch
      • Rims tires and wheel wells are given special care including degreasing, deep cleaning and a protective treatment
      • A clay bar treatment to target saps, tars, and other residues and greases that cling to your vehicles surface
      • Hand wax procedure using high quality wax that protects your vehicle from Nevada's harsh weather and other contaminants

  • Royal Restoration Package

    • A comprehensive package consisting of The 777 Detail along with a number of additional procedures and techniques aimed towards restoring and adding additional protection of your vehicle.

    • Royal Restoration Additional Services

      • Paint correction using a two-step process to remove light scratches and blemishes
      • A combination treatment of Carnauba Paste Wax and JetSeal Sealant to provide a tough long-lasting protection
      • Exterior trim restored and protected
      • Rims and any additional chrome or metal features detailed and polished
      • Engine bay deep clean and detailed
      • Included if applicable:
        • Headlight restoration
        • Upholstery and carpet damage repair

  • Additional Services

    • Theres various ways to protect your vehicle, let us assist you in finding something that fits what you need. Listed below are some of our additional services.

    • Ceramic Coatings

      • Looking for more protection for your cars paint than traditional wax and sealants? Ceramic coatings can provide extra protection from the valleys elements and contaminants for six months up to a year!

    • Vinyl Wrap and Clear Bra Installation Packages

      • 777 Can assist you in getting your vehicle wrapped with custom vinyl prints or clear bra installations. Highly customizable while also providing amazing protection for your vehicles paint, vinyl wraps are a popular service for many car enthusiast. If a vinyl wrap is not quite what suits your needs than clear bras provide a great alternative for protecting your vehicles paint.

    • Window Tinting and Glass Repair

      • We are proud to offer window tinting, windshield repair and windshield replacement services. Contact us today to talk with a team member for more information.

    • Emergency Detail Services

      • Life Happens. Whether you're a ride-share driver that had an unfortunate passenger or a parent that just does not have time to deal with the accidents, let us help out. Emergency detail services are available 24/7 365 days a year, if the incident requires immediate attention we will be there to assist you. An urgent detail fee will be charged.

    • The Royal Flush

    Let us take care of your vehicle for you. The Royal Flush is a recurring monthly program where we come to you monthly and maintain your vehicles detailing needs. Every Royal Flush package receives a Royal Restoration as an initial welcoming into our program and will continue to receive a monthly 777 Detail Package. With the Royal Flush you can rest easy knowing that your vehicle will be well taken care of.

About Us

777 was born from a love of detailing vehicles. Theres something meditative about taking your time and enjoying the process of watching a vehicle transform into a younger (cleaner) version of itself. We bring that love for the industry to each vehicle that we care for. Over the years we have refined our techniques and carefully curated the top products in the detailing world allowing us to bring that knowledge and expertise to the Las Vegas valley.

Hi, Im George and Im the founder and owner of 777 Detailing. I started 777 back in 2002 with one objective, to educate car enthusiast about great ways to clean, maintain, and protect their beautiful cars. Detailing started off as a peaceful way to enjoy my time away from work and I soon realized that I could serve my community by sharing what I enjoy doing with others. 777 was born and I've never looked back! Detailing has introduced me to so many wonderful clients and allowed me to work with some amazing cars, I look forward for the years to come and what 777 brings next..

I've always had a love for a fresh clean look on a beautiful vehicle. Detailing has been a part of my life since I was a young man and I've always enjoyed giving my cars that fresh look. I joined George and 777 in 2008 and help our clients guiding the exterior wrapping, window tinting and glass replacement processes. I enjoy creating customized exterior wraps that our clients really connect with. Vehicles are a giant canvas with plenty of room for creative design, and whether its a wrap with personal significance or one aimed towards promoting a business or brand I appreciate being apart of the process.

If you have any questions regarding automotive detailing or customization feel free to send us an email at 777@777Detail.com
